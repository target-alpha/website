About Us

A student-led organization that works towards promoting financial literacy among youth in Ontario, BC and Alberta.

Providing Opportunity For Canadian Students

At Target Alpha, we aim to engage youth in financial literacy by hosting conferences, organizing real-time competitions, and providing educational resources to Target Alpha chapters.

Over the years, we have gathered many youth leaders who run regional chapters all acrosss Canada in Ontario, Alberta, and British Columbia.

Conferences

We host various conferences featuring topics such as business and technologies.

Competitions

We organize real-time competitions and hackathons.

Resources

We provide educational resources to regional chapters, spreading knowledge to students.

Stock Trading Competition

Target Alpha Canada's newest Stock Trading Competetion convenes students from all across Canada.

Target Alpha's Annual Stock Trading Competition is back! The Target Alpha Stock Trading competition is a 3-month financial markets simulation game for all high school and university students. This year’s competition will be held on Investopedia’s stock simulator website.

This year's competition runs from December 22nd, 2021 to March 22nd, 2022. No previous knowledge or experience is required! Participants must register by December 20th, 2021

Regional Executive

Do you want to be a part of Target Alpha Canada's Regional Executive Teams?

As a Target Alpha Regional Executive, your expertise and experience will be essential to the expansion and development of this organization. This program is a great opportunity for you to get involved in the world of finance, establish unique connections with like-minded youth and industry professionals, earn volunteer hours, and gain skills you will continue to use throughout post-secondary and beyond. We expect your highest degree of performance.

In order to apply, you must currently be in grades 9-12 or Secondary III-V (for Quebec). All applications are due December 15, 2021 by 11:59 PM EST.
