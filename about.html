Providing Opportunity For Canadian Students

At Target Alpha, we aim to engage youth in financial literacy by hosting conferences, organizing real-time competitions, and providing educational resources to Target Alpha chapters.

Over the years, we have gathered many youth leaders who run regional chapters all acrosss Canada in Ontario, Alberta, and British Columbia.

Conferences

We host various conferences featuring topics such as business and technologies.

Competitions

We organize real-time competitions and hackathons.

Resources

We provide educational resources to regional chapters, spreading knowledge to students.
